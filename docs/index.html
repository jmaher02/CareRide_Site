Care Ride NKY


Our Mission

Serving the Northern Kentucky region, we strive to provide compassionate and reliable transportation service for individuals with accessibility needs.






Door to Door

By offering a direct pick-up and drop-off at the individual's residence, door-to-door non-emergency medical transport not only promotes accessibility to healthcare but also provides a compassionate and supportive approach to those with medical needs, fostering a sense of ease and reassurance throughout the entire transportation process


Any Occasion

Religious services

Family events and outings

Transport to another facility


Schedule

We recommend scheduling your pick-up in advance to secure your preferred time slot. However, recognizing the occasional urgency of our client's needs, we offer same-day pickup options based on availability.
